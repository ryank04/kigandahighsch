About Kiganda High School

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Kiganda High School has been at the forefront of quality education in Uganda for over three decades.

Our History

Kiganda High School was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education to the youth of Uganda. Starting with just 50 students and 5 teachers, we have grown to become one of the leading secondary schools in the country.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the changing educational landscape. Our alumni include prominent leaders in various fields across Uganda and beyond.

Our Values

At Kiganda High School, we are guided by a set of core values that shape our educational approach:

  • Integrity and honesty in all endeavors
  • Respect for diversity and individual differences
  • Commitment to excellence in academics and character
  • Responsibility towards our community and environment
  • Innovation in teaching and learning methodologies
